## 21. Which dorms are apartment-style at UIC?

- Status: 200
- Abstained: false

### Answer

UIC doesn't have traditional apartment-style residence halls like some universities. All on-campus housing requires meal plans and follows more of a residence hall model rather than independent apartment living.

The closest options to apartment-style would be **Commons North** and **James Stukel Towers** suites. **Commons North** gives you a private bedroom with 3-6 people sharing common areas and bathrooms, which feels more independent than traditional dorms. **JST** has suite-style living where you share space with fewer people than traditional halls.

If you're specifically looking for apartment-style independence, you'll want to consider off-campus housing in **University Village/Little Italy** (closest to campus, ~$900-1,400/month for 1BR) or the **Medical District** (~$1,000-1,600/month). Off-campus typically saves $3-8k per year and gives you that apartment experience with your own kitchen and no required meal plan.

For the most current housing options and availability, contact UIC Housing at (312) 996-3000 or housing.uic.edu.
