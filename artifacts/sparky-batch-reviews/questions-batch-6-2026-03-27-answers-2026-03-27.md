## 22. What is OPT for UIC international students?

- Status: 200
- Abstained: false

### Answer

**OPT** (Optional Practical Training) is work authorization that allows international students on F-1 visas to work in the US in their field of study after graduation. At UIC, you apply for OPT through the **Office of International Services (OIS)** in **SSB 2160**.

There are two types: **Pre-completion OPT** (work while studying) and **Post-completion OPT** (work after graduation for up to 12 months, or 36 months total for STEM graduates). You must apply to OIS before your program ends and receive your Employment Authorization Document (EAD) from USCIS to begin working.

Contact **OIS at (312) 996-3121** or **ois@uic.edu** for the application process and timeline — they'll guide you through the paperwork and USCIS requirements.
