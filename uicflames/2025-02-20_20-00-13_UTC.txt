This Thursday, we’re highlighting Tonya McGowan, a UIC Athletics Hall of Famer (1995). She scored 745 points at UIC, with a 47.9% field goal percentage, leading the team with 12.5 points per game and a 52.8% shooting mark in her final season. Her #55 jersey is one of two women's basketball uniforms to be retired by UIC.
