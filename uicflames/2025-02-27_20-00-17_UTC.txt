In this #ThrowbackThursday for Black History Month, we are honoring Sherell Ford. A member of the men's basketball program from 1992 to 1995, Ford scored 2,012 career points, the third-most in Flames history. He also holds the UIC record for field goals made (790) and ranks in the top three in rebounds (3rd, 807) and blocked shots (3rd, 164). Ford was the first UIC player to be drafted into the NBA, selected 26th overall by the Seattle SuperSonics. His #24 jersey is retired at UIC.
