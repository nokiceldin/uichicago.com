Throughout Women’s History Month, we’re dedicating Thursdays to celebrating remarkable women who have shaped UIC history. Katie Schumacher-Cawley spent 14 years with UIC Volleyball including eight as head coach. She recorded 113 wins, the third-most in program history, and led the Flames to the Horizon League championship match in 2012. This past December, Coach Schumacher-Cawley became the first female head coach to win the Division I Volleyball National Championship.
