🚨 It's official: Chair Chats with Coach K has been rescheduled for Monday, April 14, at 4 p.m.!

Join @uicchancellor Marie Lynn Miranda in the Dorin Forum for a chat about leadership and success with one of the most accomplished basketball coaches in history. As coach of Duke University’s men’s basketball team from 1980 to 2022, Mike “Coach K” Krzyzewski has the most NCAA Division I victories, most Final Fours and most Olympic gold medals as head coach. He also is co-founder of the Coach K Center on Leadership and Ethics and a professor of leadership practice at Duke’s Fuqua School of Business.
