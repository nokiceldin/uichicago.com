This Thursday for Women's History Month, we are highlighting one of the best golfers in UIC history, Phon Angwarawong! The winner of the 2016 Horizon League Championship, Angwarawong became the first golfer in program history to win a conference championship. During her time in Chicago, she set program records for scoring average (77.50), birdies (45) and lowest round (71).
