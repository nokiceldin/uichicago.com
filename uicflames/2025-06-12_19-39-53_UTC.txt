Summer Student-Athlete Spotlight

William Ridley, a rising junior on @uic_swimdive, is spending his summer interning in Congressman William Timmons’ office in the Cannon House Office Building. He’s contributing to legislative research, drafting constituent correspondence, and attending committee hearings to experience law and policymaking up close. The role gives him hands-on exposure to the real-world issues he studies as a Political Science and Criminology major at UIC, while helping build the foundation for law school and a future career in public service.
