Summer Student-Athlete Spotlight

Danielle Scimeca, a member or the UIC spirit squad, is spending part of her summer studying abroad in Sydney, Australia through the Sports and Sociology program led by Dr. Meredith Wekesser.

As part of the program, Danielle and her classmates explored popular Australian sports like netball, rugby, cricket and Aussie rules football—getting the chance to play each one. They also took part in cultural excursions to Canberra, swam in the Bondi Icebergs pool, learned to surf at Bondi Beach, and visited sites honoring the Indigenous peoples of Australia.
