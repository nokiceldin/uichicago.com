ATHLETE OF THE WEEK: Cleo Lorenzo🔥 
Senior Cleo Lorenzo has been voted Athlete of the Week! 🔥 She helped lead UIC Volleyball to the Chicago Cup title with standout performances, posting 15 kills and 9 digs against DePaul and 14 kills and 13 digs in a five-set win over Loyola. She is one of the teams biggest offensive leaders and key contributors defensively 🔥🔥
