🏀✨ From moment to movement. ✨🏀
 
Women’s basketball is having its renaissance — and Chicago is helping lead what comes next.
 
This fall, Chief Dreamer Dion Dawson pulled up to UIC to kick things off, speaking with the UIC Women’s Basketball team about going for it, real commitment, and the responsibility that comes with dreaming out loud. That visit sparked something bigger.

Today, we’re proud to announce Dion’s Chicago Dream is the official philanthropic partner of UIC Women’s Basketball — two Chicago institutions coming together to build the game, care deeply for community, and chase excellence with intention.
This is about more than hoops — it’s about banners and impact, championships and character. We are dreaming big — and doing the work to match it.

Chicago doesn’t just watch moments., we build movements. ❤️💙
