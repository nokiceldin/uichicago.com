Our @uicancercenter pediatric patients went in for treatment with a surprise and left as DESIGNERS! 🎨🔥

The patients had the chance to paint the shoes that @uic_mbb coaches will wear during the Flames' Feb. 15 game against Illinois State. 

Sunday's game is part of college basketball’s annual collaboration with the @americancancersociety and @coachesvscancer to raise awareness and funds for cancer research and treatment at academic medical centers across the country. 

This game is bigger than basketball! 🏀💥Thank you for joining us all in the fight against cancer.
